Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is a refreshing and flavorful dish that showcases the vibrant flavors of fresh seafood. Originating from Latin America, this dish involves marinating raw fish or seafood in citrus juices, typically lime or lemon, which "cooks" the proteins. Mixed with various complementary ingredients, ceviche offers a delightful combination of acidity, herbs, and spices, resulting in a light and zesty culinary experience.
