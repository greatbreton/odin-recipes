Fettucini Alfredo

Description

Indulge in the creamy decadence of Fettuccine Alfredo, a classic Italian pasta dish that effortlessly marries simplicity with rich flavor. The velvety Alfredo sauce, characterized by its smooth texture and buttery taste, elegantly coats al dente fettuccine noodles. The dish is a celebration of the harmonious blend between the subtle notes of Parmesan cheese and the luxurious creaminess of the sauce. Every forkful delivers a luscious combination of textures and tastes, creating a comforting and satisfying dining experience. Fettuccine Alfredo's allure lies in its ability to transform a handful of quality ingredients into a timeless and delightful culinary masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine noodles
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Add fettuccine pasta to the boiling water and cook until al dente.
  3. Meanwhile, in a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
  4. Add heavy cream to the melted butter and bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  6. Season the Alfredo sauce with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg (optional).
  7. Drain the cooked fettuccine and add it to the Alfredo sauce.
  8. Toss the pasta in the sauce until well-coated and heated through.
  9. Serve the Fettuccine Alfredo hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and parsley if desired.
