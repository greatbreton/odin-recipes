Red Velvet Cake

Description

A delightful red velvet cake made with cream cheese frosting and red velvet crumble

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 cup buttermilk (room temperature)
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 tbsp red food coloring
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and prepare cake pans by greasing and flouring.
  2. In a bowl, mix dry ingredients: 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 1/2 cups sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp cocoa powder, and 1/2 tsp salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk wet ingredients: 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil, 1 cup buttermilk, 2 eggs, 2 tbsp red food coloring, 1 tsp vanilla extract, and 1 tsp white vinegar.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed. Don't overmix.
  5. Divide batter into prepared pans and bake for about 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow the cakes to cool in pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  7. For the cream cheese frosting, beat together 8 oz cream cheese, 1/2 cup butter, 4 cups confectioners' sugar, and 1 tsp vanilla extract until smooth.
  8. Frost the cooled cakes with the cream cheese frosting.
  9. Optional: Decorate with chopped nuts.
  10. Slice, serve, and enjoy your homemade red velvet cake!
